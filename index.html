About the program

The public site will grow into a stable archive of figures, summaries, and links to deeper work—written for students, faculty, and anyone who cares about pond health without a wall of jargon.

  • Present the work as legitimate research through MESA.
  • Share what we study, how we study it, and what we have learned.
  • Make results and discoveries easy to browse (by pond, topic, or time).
  • Explain why habitat quality matters for organisms in and around each pond.
